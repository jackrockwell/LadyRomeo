Asha Romeo

Asha Romeo is a Boulder native, and a sophomore at CU Boulder’s School of Music. Music has always been a part of her life, with both her parents and oldest brother being musicians. She is a soprano in the classical sense, but has been able to adapt to and perform a wide variety of genres. For example, she has played Joanne Jefferson in Rent the musical, Sally Bowles in Cabaret, and the Witch in Into The Woods, while also playing a chorus member in the operas La Traviata by Verdi and It’s A Wonderful Life by Jake Heggie. In the past she has composed and performed original music on her own, but is thrilled to have a creative team. She is looking forward to growing and exploring, musically, what Lady Romeo is.

Gerald Robinson

Gerald Robinson was born and raised in Boulder, Colorado and knows how to drive in the snow. Gerald is a Junior at the University of Colorado earning a BA in Music Technology and a Minor in Math. He studies classical guitar, but also composes, performs and produces original material for his band Lady Romeo. He has a strong passion for utilizing electronics like synthesizers and sound processing programs like Supercollider and Ableton, which have enhanced Lady Romeo’s creative process. Gerald has had the pleasure of being a part of jazz, electronic and classical ensembles at CU. He plans to continue his education in Music technology, and to compose music for television, movies and everything in between.

Jack Rockwell

Jack Rockwell is a Colorado native, and currently a junior at the University of Colorado Boulder studying art practices. He has been playing bass for the past nine years, and has been a part of multiple bands including, 5000, and Toy Dinosaur. He started his first band with Gerald Robinson in 2011, and they haven’t stopped making music since, ever expanding their musical repertoire and abilities to adapt and perform to multiple genres.

Rowan Woodbury

Rowan Woodbury is a Denver based percussionist who has been playing the drum set for ten years. He is also highly experienced in concert, mallet, and marching percussion. He has played with numerous groups, including the rock trio Nocturne, two seasons touring with The Battalion drum and bugle corps, and one season touring with The Blue Knights indoor percussion. Rowan is currently a music education student at the University of Colorado, Boulder, where he studies with Dr. Doug Walter. In his free time he enjoys hiking, climbing, and reading.
